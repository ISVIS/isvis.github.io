Touch Monitor


Windows App

Description
Ensure touch screens and self-checkouts are cleaned regularly to reduce the spread of viral infections.
Protect your customers with scheduled cleaning and by indicating where screens were previously touched.

Features
Cleaning schedule with configurable touch counters and cleaning intervals
Cleaning notifications with optional screen unlocking with a PIN code
Touch point indications that persist until cleared after cleaning
Attendant or customer assigned cleaning responsibility
Notifications with schedule updates and suggestions
Touch point style and animation settings
Screen size and theme configuration
Works with desktop and browser apps
